about me

After researching a range of sectors for investment, I was constantly amazed by Tech, both domestically and abroad. I enjoyed understanding the strategies and dynamics of tech companies and discovering how unique the industry is in a lot of ways (open source for example).

I got more and more into coding after building some of the systems my old team used for analytics and reporting (via Microsoft VBA). I really enjoyed these things put to use. I took the Web Development Immersive through General Assembly to fast track my learning and haven't looked back since.

I'm now a junior web developer. I get a kick out of building things that people can use. I love looking under the hood, problem solving and being challenged. I am excited to constantly develop my skills and grow as a developer.

I enjoy working on projects with others to either teach or learn from.

Some of the skills I've picked up include: JavaScript, Ruby, JQuery, React, Rails, Backbone, Ajax, PostGreSQL, Jasmine, TDD, Git, Project Management and Researching.
